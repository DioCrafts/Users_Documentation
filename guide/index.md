# What is AXIOM?

AXIOM is a workspace for **investigation and analysis**. It takes data from many
places — spreadsheets, live feeds, documents — and turns it into a single,
connected picture you can explore, question, and act on.

Instead of jumping between systems and copying facts into a notebook, you work
with **objects** (people, organizations, accounts, vessels, places, transactions,
documents…) that are linked to one another. You can search for one, open its full
profile, follow its connections, see it on a map, ask the built-in AI about it,
raise an alert, run a case, and write up your findings — all in one place, and all
recorded.

## What you can do with it

- **Find anything fast.** A single search box finds objects by name or detail, and
  jumps you to any screen in the app.
- **Understand a network.** Lay out connections on an interactive graph, find how
  two entities are linked, surface who they have in common, and rank the most
  central players.
- **Follow movement.** Plot positions and routes on a map, replay them over time,
  and detect patterns such as vessels travelling together or lingering in an area.
- **Spot what matters.** Rules watch your data and raise alerts when something
  matches; a board helps you triage them.
- **Run governed cases.** Each investigation moves through defined stages, with
  approvals where they're needed and an audit trail throughout.
- **Ask questions in plain language.** The Copilot answers from your data and shows
  you the sources behind every claim.
- **Bring in new data.** Import a spreadsheet, connect a feed, build a pipeline to
  clean and shape it.
- **Write it up.** Capture quick notes, then build collaborative reports with live
  objects and charts, and export them as a PDF dossier.

## How AXIOM is organized

Everything in AXIOM is reached from a **sidebar of modules** grouped by what you're
trying to do:

| Group | Modules | For… |
|---|---|---|
| **Workspace** | Library | Everything you and your team have built |
| **Explore & Model** | Ontology, Entity Resolution | Your data model and de-duplication |
| **Analyze** | Graph, Geospatial, Dashboard, Ad-hoc analysis | Making sense of the data |
| **Decide & Act** | Alerts, Approvals, AI agents, Reports, Notepad, Composer, Watchlists | Acting on what you find |
| **Integrate** | Sources, Connectors, Pipelines, Code, Models | Getting data in and shaping it |
| **Govern** | Governance & Audit, Catalog | Policies, access, and the audit trail |

You don't have to use all of it. Most people live in a handful of modules and pin
those to their sidebar. See [Finding your way around](/guide/navigation) for how to
tailor the sidebar to you.

## A few ideas that run through the whole app

A small number of principles shape how AXIOM behaves everywhere. They're worth
knowing up front, and explained fully in [Core concepts](/guide/concepts):

- **You only see what you're cleared for.** Hidden data is simply absent — you
  won't even know it exists. This keeps everyone's view safe by default.
- **AI proposes, a person decides.** The AI and automated rules can *suggest*
  actions, but a human always approves before anything changes.
- **Important changes need a second pair of eyes.** Sensitive actions and case
  steps require a different person to approve them.
- **Everything is recorded.** Who did what, and when, is captured in a tamper-evident
  audit trail you can review and verify.
- **You can look back in time.** Because history is kept, you can view the data as
  it stood at an earlier date.
