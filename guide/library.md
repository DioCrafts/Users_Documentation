# The Library

The **Library** is where everything your team has built lives in one place:
pipelines, Composer apps, code, reports, models, and notes. It's the cross-cutting
shelf for work products — sorted newest-first so the latest work is easy to find.

## What you'll see

A filter bar across the top and a grid of cards below. Each card represents one work
product and shows its icon, name, owner, classification, and date. Click a card to
open that item in its own editor.

### Filtering

- **Search** by name.
- **Type chips** — narrow to a kind of work product: **Pipelines**, **Composer**
  (apps), **Code**, **Reports**, **Models**, or **Notes**. Each chip shows a count.
  **All** shows everything.
- **Recent / Mine** — everything newest-first, or just what you created.
- **Limit to active project** — when you have a project in focus, tick this to show
  only that project's work products.

The filters work together and don't reset one another, so you can combine, say,
"Reports" + "Mine" + a search term.

## The Library vs. a project's workspace

The Library and a project's workspace are two views of the same content:

- **The Library** (this screen) shows work products across *everything* you can see.
- **A project's workspace** (inside a [project](/guide/projects)) shows only that
  project's work products and lets you add or remove which pipelines and models
  belong to it.

When you're inside a project's workspace, each card may have a small **remove**
control to unlink it from the project. Removing it only undoes the organizational
link — it doesn't delete the item.

## Recipes

**Find the latest version of something.**
Open the Library → click the **Pipelines** chip → leave the sort on **Recent** → the
newest pipeline is at the top. Click it to open and see who owns it and when it last
changed.

**Find all your own work.**
Switch to **Mine** → optionally type a keyword → every report, note, app, and
pipeline you created is listed.

**Search across all analysis.**
Type a term such as "sanctions" → results span reports, notes, and other products
that match. Add a type chip to focus.
