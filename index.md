## Who this guide is for

This guide is for the people who **use** AXIOM day to day — analysts, reviewers,
investigators, and administrators. It explains every screen and feature from the
point of view of *what you see and what you can do*, with step-by-step recipes for
common tasks. No prior training required.

If you are brand new, start with [First steps](/guide/getting-started) and then read
[Core concepts](/guide/concepts) — those two pages give you everything you need to
find your footing. After that, dip into whichever module you need.
